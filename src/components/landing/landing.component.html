How to Play

  1. Write your names and pick a color for each player
  2. Roll the dice
  3. Pick a side of a number that equals the first die multiplied by the second die. For example: if you roll 3 and 2, you can draw a line beside any 6 square, since 3 x 2 = 6
  4. If you draw the line that creates a square around a number, that number is filled in with your color, and you score a point.
  5. If the number you're looking for isn't available, you have to skip your turn.
  6. Play until there are no options left for any players. Highest score wins!
  7. If you want to play again, simply reset the game.
